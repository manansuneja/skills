# references/ - Index

References are examples and style/context samples the owner wants skills to learn from. Put files here
when they show how the owner writes, thinks, or structures work, but are not themselves a reusable
blank template.

Good examples:

- `meeting-minutes-example.md` - a real or sanitized example of meeting notes.
- `offer-brief-example.md` - a finished offer brief whose structure or tone should influence future offer briefs.
- `client-update-example.md` - a past client update that shows voice and level of detail.
- `plan-example.md` - a plan example with the right planning altitude.

Use lower-kebab-case filenames. Update this index when adding, renaming, or removing references.
