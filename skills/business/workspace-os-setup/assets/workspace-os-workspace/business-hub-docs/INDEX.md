# {{DOCS_ROOT}}/ - Index

The actual business work. Capture lives here; agents read it to build on context instead of starting over.

| Item | What it is | Open when |
|---|---|---|
| [business-profile.md](business-profile.md) | The single source of truth: what the business or project is and why | You need the north star or to ground a decision |
| [meetings/](meetings/INDEX.md) | One folder per meeting - summaries plus raw source archives | Looking up what was said / decided |
| [outcomes/](outcomes/INDEX.md) | Insights, recommendations, prioritization, scope cuts, and next-step plans | Looking up what we concluded from context |
| [offers/](offers/INDEX.md) | offer brief hierarchy: area/package -> offer -> client stories | Defining or breaking down buildable scope |
| [decisions/](decisions/INDEX.md) | Decisions and their rationale | Asking "why did we do it this way?" |
| [brand-and-delivery/](brand-and-delivery/INDEX.md) | UX flows, brand and delivery considerations, accessibility notes, and trade-offs | Thinking through business experience |
| [metrics/](metrics/INDEX.md) | Metric reads, funnels, experiments, and quantitative evidence | Asking what the data says |
| [plans/](plans/INDEX.md) | Now/next/later plans, releases, sequencing, and dependencies | Planning what happens when |
| [client-comms/](client-comms/INDEX.md) | Updates, briefs, launch notes, client summaries, and alignment messages | Communicating business work |

New type of artifact (research, prototypes, comms)? Make a folder here, give it an `INDEX.md`, and
add it to the root [INDEX.md](../INDEX.md). See [apply-workspace-struct](../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/apply-workspace-struct.md).
