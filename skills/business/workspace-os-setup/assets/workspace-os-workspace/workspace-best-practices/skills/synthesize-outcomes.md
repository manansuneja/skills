# Skill: synthesize-outcomes

> **For you and your agents:** This skill controls recommendation structure and decision quality.
> Ask the Workspace Chief to adapt it to your preferred format or level of rigor.

Turn context into a durable recommendation, priority set, trade-off analysis, or next-step decision.

Before drafting, check [outcome.md](../templates/outcome.md) and relevant
[references](../references/INDEX.md).

## Method

- Ground claims in the workspace profile, source notes, prior decisions, or explicit input.
- Separate evidence from judgment.
- Prioritize by desired outcome rather than option volume.
- Make uncertainty, trade-offs, and next actions explicit.
- Create follow-on artifacts only when the decision warrants them.

## Filing

Write to `{{DOCS_ROOT}}/outcomes/<MMM-DD-YYYY>-<topic>.md`, update the
[outcomes index](../../{{DOCS_ROOT}}/outcomes/INDEX.md), and apply
[apply-workspace-struct](../../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/apply-workspace-struct.md). Keep chat to a
short handoff with the path and top recommendation.
