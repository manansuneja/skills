# Skill: summarize-notes

> **For you and your agents:** This skill controls summary structure and emphasis. Ask the Workspace
> Chief to change it, or add a preferred example under `../references/`.

Turn raw, messy notes into a summary a busy person can act on quickly and an agent can reuse later.

Before drafting, check [meeting-summary.md](../templates/meeting-summary.md) and relevant
[references](../references/INDEX.md).

## Method

- Lead with the answer: short summary and decisions first.
- Separate facts from interpretation; never invent a decision.
- Preserve important context, tension, and evidence.
- Make actions specific: owner, verb, and date when known.
- Flag content that deserves its own outcome, decision, or category-specific artifact.

## Filing

If notes are loose, archive them unchanged under
`{{DOCS_ROOT}}/meetings/<MMM-DD-YYYY>-<short-title>/raw/raw-notes.md`, then write `summary.md` beside
`raw/`. Remove a duplicate inbox copy only after the archive exists. Update the
[meetings index](../../{{DOCS_ROOT}}/meetings/INDEX.md) and apply
[apply-workspace-struct](../../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/apply-workspace-struct.md).
