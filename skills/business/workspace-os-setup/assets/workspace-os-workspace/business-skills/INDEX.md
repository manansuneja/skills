# business-skills/ - Index

Business skills are repeatable business judgment: how this owner wants a kind of business work done. Agents
apply them; the owner refines them over time. As you do more reps, move taste from your head into these
files. Business skills are the neutral *how*; the personas in
[agents/sub-agents/](../agents/sub-agents/INDEX.md) are the *who* that apply them.

New here? Start with [START_HERE.md](START_HERE.md). It explains the base skills and how to update
them by talking to the agent.

Workspace setup rules are intentionally separate and live under
[_workspace_setup_docs/](../_workspace_setup_docs/INDEX.md). Leave
[apply-workspace-struct](../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/apply-workspace-struct.md) and
[manage-workspace-skills](../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/manage-workspace-skills.md) there unless
the workspace structure itself is changing.
