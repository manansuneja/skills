# Skill: brand-and-delivery

Turn business context into durable brand and delivery considerations: UX flows, interaction trade-offs,
accessibility notes, content/brand and delivery questions, and review feedback.

## Templates and references

Before using the default structure, check:

- [business-practices/templates/brand-delivery-note.md](../business-practices/templates/brand-delivery-note.md) for the current brand and delivery note template.
- [business-practices/references/](../business-practices/references/INDEX.md) for Owner-provided brand and delivery review, UX flow, or usability examples.

If the owner adds a better brand and delivery template or reference example, follow that and keep this skill's
judgment rules.

## How to do it well

- Ground brand and delivery claims in business context, user feedback, data, or explicit business judgment.
- Capture trade-offs, not just preferences.
- Link buildable scope to the relevant offer brief folder.
- Keep chat short; write the durable brand and delivery note to `{{DOCS_ROOT}}/brand-and-delivery/`.

## Filing

Write to `{{DOCS_ROOT}}/brand-and-delivery/<MMM-DD-YYYY>-<topic>.md`, update
[the brand and delivery index](../{{DOCS_ROOT}}/brand-and-delivery/INDEX.md), and apply
[apply-workspace-struct](../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/apply-workspace-struct.md).
