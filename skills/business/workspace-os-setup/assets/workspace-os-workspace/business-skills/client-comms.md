# Skill: client-comms

Turn business context into clear client communication: exec updates, client updates, launch notes,
status briefs, alignment messages, and decision summaries.

## Templates and references

Before using the default structure, check:

- [business-practices/templates/client-communication.md](../business-practices/templates/client-communication.md) for the current
  client communication template.
- [business-practices/references/](../business-practices/references/INDEX.md) for Owner-provided examples of updates, briefs, launch notes,
  client summaries, or voice/tone samples.

If the owner adds a better communication template or reference example, follow that and keep this skill's
audience and source-grounding rules.

## How to do it well

- Tailor the message to the audience and decision needed.
- Be concise, specific, and source-grounded.
- Do not expose internal workspace mechanics unless the client needs them.
- Link the source artifacts so future agents can see why the communication was written.

## Filing

Write to `{{DOCS_ROOT}}/client-comms/<MMM-DD-YYYY>-<topic>.md`, update
[the client-comms index](../{{DOCS_ROOT}}/client-comms/INDEX.md), and apply
[apply-workspace-struct](../_workspace_setup_docs/skills/apply-workspace-struct.md).
