## How To Apply A Profile

1. Choose the docs root: `business-hub-docs/` for a standing business hub, `project-docs/` for a
   client project.
2. Create any profile-specific folders with an `INDEX.md`.
3. Add or adapt starter skills only when the workflow is likely to repeat.
4. Update the root `INDEX.md`, docs-root `INDEX.md`, `business-skills/INDEX.md`,
   `agents/sub-agents/INDEX.md`, and `agents/workspace-chief.md` routing.
5. Keep names lower-kebab-case and avoid overfitting before the owner has used the workspace.

## Business Categories

- **Creative studio or interior design:** emphasize clients, projects, proposals, inspiration,
  vendors, installation/delivery, brand-and-delivery, and client-comms.
- **Consulting, coaching, or professional services:** emphasize offers, clients, calls/meetings,
  outcomes, knowledge-base, proposals, client-comms, and plans.
- **Agency or freelance practice:** emphasize client pipeline, projects, delivery, briefs, approvals,
  assets, operations, and client-comms.
- **Ecommerce or product business:** emphasize offers/catalog, marketing, metrics, operations,
  suppliers, plans, and decisions.
- **Local service business:** emphasize clients, jobs/projects, operations, SOPs, finance, marketing,
  reviews, and scheduling.
- **Knowledge or education business:** emphasize knowledge-base, research, notes, offers,
  content/marketing, and study plans.

## Primary Use Profiles

### Client Management

Best for businesses that mostly need to track clients, projects, proposals, communications, and
delivery.

Add folders:

- `clients/`
- `projects/`
- `proposals/`
- `intake/`

Starter skills to add or emphasize:

- `client-management.md`
- `client-intake.md`
- `project-delivery.md`
- Existing `client-comms.md`

Specialists to add or emphasize:

- `client-manager.md`
- `project-delivery-partner.md`
