# references/ — Index

> **For you and your agents:** Reusable examples, source material, product principles, voice, and
> style guidance. Tell the Chief PM what each reference should influence.

References are examples and style/context samples the PM wants skills to learn from. Put files here
when they show how the PM writes, thinks, or structures work, but are not themselves a reusable
blank template.

Good examples:

- `meeting-minutes-example.md` - a real or sanitized example of meeting notes.
- `prd-example.md` - a finished PRD whose shape or tone should influence future PRDs.
- `stakeholder-update-example.md` - a past update that shows voice and level of detail.
- `roadmap-example.md` - a roadmap example with the right planning altitude.

Use lower-kebab-case filenames. Update this index when adding, renaming, or removing references.
